Description

This is an amazing recipe for lasagna that stems from ancient Italy. You will never be able to make this particular lasagna, as the primary ingredient no longer exists. The primary ingredient is an ancient bacteria which helps ferment the cheese inside the lasagna as it is baking. The bacteria was wiped out along with the Romans, after the library of Alexandria was burned to the ground by enthusiastic christian barbarians. The last time someone attempted to make this lasagna, in the Wu-Han lab, a pandemic arose. Please do not attempt to make this lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Ancient Bacteria
  • Ground Meat of Choice
  • Ragu
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • A Positive Attitude
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Soften noodles in hot water
  2. Layer all ingredients in a rectangular pan
  3. Bake at 350Deg for two hours
  4. Pray to whatever diety you believe in that you don't start a pandemic
  5. Serve hot and Enjoy!
