A Hotdog To Die For

Return to Homepage

Description

Seriously, this is just a plain ol' hotdog, but it can kill you. You can spice it up anyway you like. This recipe is almost impossible to mess up, unless of course you survive. If you can boil water, cut toppings, and acquire poion, then you can make a hotdog to die for. I recommend a natural casing hotdog with mustard, a Claussen pickle, hot peppers, and ricin poison, on a seaseme seed bun.

Ingredients

  • A package of your favorite hotdog brand
  • Buns of your choice
  • Poisono of your choice
  • Any condiments or toppings you like

Steps

  1. Cut or dice up any toppings you would like
  2. Boil or grill Hotdog until warm throughout
  3. Place warm hotdog in bun and cover in toppings or condiments
  4. Serve warm and Enjoy!
